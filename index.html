Key Takeaways

  • Quality over quantity: Cold email positive rate jumped 7-8x (0.12% → ~1%) through intent-qualified targeting — 6Sense, web visitors, and traffic growth signals.
  • Meta ads funnel improved 3x: Meeting-to-opportunity rate went from 12.5% to 36%. Better pre-qualification and targeting are working.
  • Meta pipeline 7x: $40K → $286K. Onesizebeauty ($56K), Saalt ($50K), and Pampeano ($29.6K — CLOSED) are the top deals from ads.
  • First closed deal: Pampeano closed at $29,627 from Meta Ads — validates the paid ads funnel end-to-end.
  • Email pipeline 4x: $44K → $170K. Mate the Label ($50K) is the largest email-sourced deal. 4 more TBD deals awaiting PLD.
  • Partner channel validated: Asendia co-brand campaign generated 90 positives from 4,960 sends — proves warm lists dramatically outperform cold.
  • Q1 pipeline at $731K: 36.6% of $2M target through Month 2. Raycon ($230K) closed lost — biggest single pipeline loss. Need $1.27M in March to hit target. 4 deals waiting on PLD + new campaign structure should help.

March Priorities

Data-driven action plan based on February performance.

1
Restructure Meta Ads Campaigns

Splitting into separate Testing and Scaling campaigns. Higher budget allocated to Scaling campaign for proven angles — should drive better CPL and more qualified meetings.

2
Build Robust Follow-Up System

Leads are coming in but follow-up gaps are causing deal leaks. Building a structured multi-channel follow-up method: email sequences, LinkedIn touchpoints, and phone call outreach.

3
Launch Nurture Campaigns

Two nurture tracks: email nurture for warm leads not yet meeting-ready, and paid ads retargeting nurture to stay top-of-mind with engaged prospects.

4
Launch Generic OpenBorder Campaign

Full-TAM campaign targeting the complete OpenBorder addressable market. Broader reach to fill the top of funnel alongside intent-qualified campaigns.

5
Close PLD-Pending Deals

Conquering, Yotoplay (1.9M traffic), Samplize, Ripndip — all waiting on PLD. Deals will be created once PLDs are delivered. Potential $80-100K+.

6
Scale High Intl Traffic T2

T1 at 3.24% positive rate = best cold campaign. T2 scales proven angle to ~1,500 sends. Already drafted Feb 27.
